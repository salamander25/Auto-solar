What women ask us before they start

Do I need experience?

No. We specialize in first-time athletes and teach you lifting, posing, and show-day flow from scratch.

How often do we meet?

Weekly check-ins (video or in-person), posing labs, and optional Saturday beach conditioning in Orange County.

Will I be too busy?

We design around work, kids, and travel. Expect 4-5 training days with realistic meal prep options.

What about health?

We prioritize sleep, digestion, and hormones. If labs are needed, we connect you with our partner clinicians.

Do you help with suits?

Yes. We recommend colors for your complexion, connect you with trusted suit makers, and review fit on video.

Is there a community?

Yes! Private group chat, show-day buddies, and meetups across LA/OC/SD so you never feel alone.
